Who we are

Who is Antioch Church Durham?

Antioch Church Durham is part of a global family of churches that are Spirit-led, biblically based, and committed to the person of Jesus, His rule and reign, and His values lived out in community.

We want to see transformation in personal lives, families, neighborhoods, and every sphere of society in Durham and beyond for the glory of God.

We are a people who are passionate about Jesus and His purposes in the earth.

How we do church

Five Circles of Healthy Church

We want our relationship with Jesus to affect and transform every area of our lives. These five circles help us grow in a holistic way as disciples who love God, love one another, and live on mission.

Psalm 27:4, 8 Personal Devotional Life
Matthew 28:18–20 Life on Life Discipleship
Acts 2:42–47 House to House
Acts 2:46–47 Church Gathered
Acts 1:8 Witness in the World

Instead of focusing on only one circle, we hope to grow by embracing all five: deep devotional life, discipleship, community, corporate gatherings, and mission where God has planted us.
